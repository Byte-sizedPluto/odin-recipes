Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a delectable dessert known for its creamy, velvety texture and rich, indulgent flavor. It typically features a sweet and buttery graham cracker crust topped with a smooth, dense layer of cream cheese filling. Cheesecakes can be customized with various toppings and flavors, making them a beloved treat for dessert enthusiasts worldwide.
