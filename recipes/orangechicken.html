Orange Chicken

Orange chicken is a popular Chinese-American dish known for its sweet and tangy flavor. It features bite-sized pieces of crispy, battered chicken coated in a vibrant orange sauce made from fresh orange juice, sugar, vinegar, and soy sauce. The result is a delicious combination of crispy textures and bright citrus notes, making it a favorite choice in Chinese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • chicken broth
  • Granulated sugar
  • Rice vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Cornstarch
  1. Season and fry chicken
  2. Prep the orange sauce
  3. Cook the sauce
  4. Combine the chicken and the sauce
