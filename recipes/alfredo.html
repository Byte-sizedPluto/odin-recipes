Alfredo

Alfredo sauce is a luxurious Italian pasta sauce renowned for its rich and creamy texture. It combines butter, heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, and sometimes garlic to create a decadent flavor profile. This velvety sauce beautifully coats pasta, turning a simple dish into a gourmet delight. Alfredo sauce is celebrated for its ability to transform any pasta meal into a truly indulgent culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic and Onion Powder
  • Parmesan Cheese
  1. Prep Ingredients
  2. Melt butter on medium heat
  3. Add garlic and onion powder
  4. Pour heavy cream
  5. Simmer and then add parmesan cheese
  6. Taste the sauce and add sasonings as needed
  7. Add more parmesan cheese
  8. Combine with previously cooked pasta
